Many people have money sitting in a bank account seek ways to invest. Comparing to the rather unstable stock market, Peer to Peer(P2P) would be a great choice. P2P lending is the method of lending money to individuals or businesses through online services. On the investor's side, the investment return is much higher than bank CD account, and much safer than stock market.

I used data sets from the two industry lead companies: Prosper and Lending Club. I analyzed the P2P lending data for the past 10 years and run feature engineering and feature selection to construct a regression model to predict the interest rate of a given loan.
